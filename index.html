Scope and Limitations — Research Interface V1

The Λ sector is kinematic (phenomenological): Λ_eff(t) is prescribed, not derived from a Lagrangian or action principle.

No perturbation analysis is implemented. Linear and non-linear matter/radiation perturbations are absent.

No mass-evolution dynamics M(t) is included. M enters through the static capacity Ṽ only. Strong sensitivity to M reflects the absence of dynamical mass evolution in V1.

v(t) = t^q is a minimal one-parameter slicing ansatz. No derivation from the Cauchy-Regge or spin-foam graph structure is provided. [V1 placeholder]

The UV regulator (+0.5 or V floor 1e-8) is a numerical and phenomenological regularization, not a derivation from full LQC microphysics. It prevents singular coupling at t → 0. [physical floor requires Planck-cell counting — V1 placeholder]

Growing+decay mode avoids Big Rip: Λ → 1 asymptotically. No phantom runaway occurs at finite time.

Auto-fit rescales η by S = 0.69/Ω_Λ_model in a single step, then re-runs the full integration. The result approaches but may not exactly reach Ω_Λ=0.69 due to nonlinearity. Iterative bisection is not implemented in V1.

DESI 2024 indicated mild w_a < 0 tension. If DESI 2026+ confirms w ≈ −1 constant, the decay mode becomes observationally less motivated. [DESI 2026 data not available as of V1 release]

RK2 midpoint integrator: O(dt²) accuracy. dt = 1.6×10⁻⁴, N ≥ 7000 guaranteed to detect a=1 crossing. No silent fallback at epoch of today.

β (capacity suppression exponent) modifies only the suppression term in the Λ driver: Λ = 1 + η·f(t) / Ṽ(t)^β. β=1 reproduces V1 exactly. β<1 weakens suppression (larger coupling effect). β>1 strengthens suppression (smaller coupling effect). β does not affect Ṽ definition, regulator logic, Ω_Λ computation, or the Friedmann background.

Driver structure limitation (V1): The current smooth single-phase driver form couples amplitude and late-time slope (w₀–wₐ linkage). Increasing |wₐ| generically induces a deviation of w₀ from −1 within this mapping. Pivot-anchored or multi-phase accretion drivers are not implemented in V1. [structural limitation of baseline mapping]
